# Roadmap

This section outlines TRIBE's current status, future goals, and business model that supports the platform's growth and sustainability.

## Current Status

TRIBE is already gaining momentum:

- **Intuition Mainnet Integration**: TRIBE has been successfully integrated on the Intuition Mainnet, marking a significant milestone in the platform's development and adoption.

## 2026 Goals

TRIBE aims to achieve significant user growth in 2026:

- **100,000 Users**: Hit 100,000 active users on the platform
- **Platform Expansion**: Continue building features and improving user experience
- **Community Growth**: Foster a thriving community of meme token creators and traders

## 2027 Vision

TRIBE's long-term vision for 2027 includes ambitious targets:

- **$100 Million Trading Volume**: Achieve $100 million in cumulative trading volume across all tokens launched on the platform
- **L2 Ecosystem Partnerships**: Establish partnerships with 3+ Layer 2 (L2) ecosystems to expand TRIBE's reach and accessibility
- **Verified Projects**: Build a thriving community of verified meme coin projects with strong fundamentals and active communities

## Business Model

TRIBE sustains itself through multiple revenue streams that ensure platform growth and community incentives:

### Transaction Fees

- **2% Fee on Trades**: A 2% fee on all buy/sell transactions ensures affordability while supporting platform operations
- **Treasury Routing**: Fees are automatically routed to the treasury, supporting platform development and sustainability

### Premium Tools

- **$TRUST Staking for Verification**: Creators can stake $TRUST tokens to verify their projects, adding credibility and trust to their tokens
- **Marketing Features**: Premium marketing features available for creators to promote their tokens and grow their communities

### Partnerships

- **L2 Network Revenue Sharing**: Revenue-sharing agreements with Layer 2 networks provide mutual benefits
- **DeFi Protocol Integration**: Partnerships with DeFi protocols expand TRIBE's ecosystem and utility

### Revenue Projections

This diversified business model projects **$500k in revenue by 2027**, supporting:
- Platform growth and development
- Community incentives and rewards
- Infrastructure improvements
- Strategic partnerships

## Growth Strategy

TRIBE's growth strategy focuses on:

1. **User Acquisition**: Attracting creators and traders through accessible token launch tools
2. **Platform Development**: Continuously improving features based on community feedback
3. **Ecosystem Expansion**: Building partnerships with L2 networks and DeFi protocols
4. **Community Building**: Fostering a vibrant community of meme token enthusiasts
